About Me

Hi, It's Me!

I started my early IT career by obtaining a Diploma in PC Engineering from Brooklyn City College in Pretoria, South Africa, where I subsequently owned and managed a company providing IT Services to embassies, offices and private clients. As an Assistant Educator for Computer Application Technology on high school level, I facilitated knowledge and skills transfer, mainly in computer network and fundamental web design.
In 2017, seeking in-depth knowledge with regard to programming, I enrolled with CS50’s ‘Harvard University's introduction to the intellectual enterprises of Computer Science and the art of programming’ through the online learning platform edX. Despite this course being challenging for me as my personal entry-level course, I succeeded with 94 out of 100 possible points. Through this course, I gained a robust understanding of computer science and programming and was taught how to solve programming problems efficiently and how to conceptualize, implement, and present a major programming project. The languages include C, Python, SQL, and JavaScript plus CSS and HTML.
Going through the CS50 course has increased my desire to further develop my theoretical knowledge and practical skills in software and web development, leading me to start with both CS50's ‘Web Programming with Python and JavaScript’ as well as freeCodeCamp exercises, which I believe have the right curriculum mapping and provide the right tools for full stack web development. Meanwhile, I am continuously enhancing my knowledge and skills as to Python, C and other programming languages.
